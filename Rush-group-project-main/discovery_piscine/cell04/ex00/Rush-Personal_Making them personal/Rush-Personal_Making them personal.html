Making them personal

UN goal 3-Ensure healthy lives and promote well-being for all at all ages
The UN goal 3 looks at ensuring health for people, and making sure everyone has access to health and a medical system. This is a big deal, especially in the United Nations, because so many people don’t have access, or can’t afford access to the medical system, or live in such a state of poverty that there is no way they would be able to get the medical help they need. This is a big problem, as every human deserves a right to a medical system, and no one should have to suffer in pain, or even die simply because they were unable to afford the medical care they needed.

Why is this a problem and how does it affect me?
No matter where you live, or what medical system you have access to, ensuring the health is afforded to everyone is important. Having access to a healthy enviroment allows for strong growth, and reduces death and disease. For countries without access to a medical instiutaion, poverty and death rates increase, and this leads to a lack of a healthy enviroment, and poor health for the next generations to come.


Why am I passionate about this goal?
UN goal 3 is all about providing health and promoting wellbeing for allx. When I was 11, my sister was diagnosed with AML leakumia, and has spent her life recovering, the side affects of the cancer destroying her life. When I was 14, I lost my grandfather to cancer after a long and hard battle. This year, my father was hit by a truck while on his bike, and almost lost his life.
My point? My family has a history of medical needs, I have seen what happens when family's don't have access to the medical system they need, and I have also seen what happens when people are able to get medical attention. Because of my past, I am able to have empathy towards those who do not have access to a medical system, and this leads me to be passionate about this topic.
